Throughout school I explored many passions such as building robots for the Vibots, leading a mathlete team, and joining a chess club. In 2019 I graduated from Brandon High with Honors, testing the waters with college before quickly realizing it wasn't for me. I've always come up with ways to support myself without working at a conventional job. My favorite method was creating a service to help players improve in a video game I loved. Through this service I was able to travel across the country, have a ton of fun, and eventually move in with my partner! We now live in a beautiful small town in Massachussets and it's a dream come true.
Now that i'm settled in to a new home with my partner, it's time to continue with the next step of life: Turning a passion into a career. Analyzing stats and data has always been amazing, i'd love to do it for a living! Unfortunately staring at solely numbers all day wasn't for me, but fortunately Mehak opened my eyes to the world of Growth Marketing. In this field I can analyze data and hypothesize the best methods to improve results, sounds like a second dream come true!
